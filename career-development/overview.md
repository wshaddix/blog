# Career Development

Technical skills get you hired. Career skills help you grow, lead, and make an impact.

## What's Covered

### Career Ladder

The path from Individual Contributor to executive leadership:

- [Individual Contributor](/career-development/career-ladder/individual-contributor) - Technical excellence
- [Manager](/career-development/career-ladder/manager) - Leading a team
- [Director](/career-development/career-ladder/director) - Leading multiple teams
- [VP](/career-development/career-ladder/vp) - Leading an organization

### People Management

Skills for anyone managing engineers:

- [One-on-Ones](/career-development/people-management/one-on-ones) - Effective 1:1 meetings
- [Giving Feedback](/career-development/people-management/feedback) - Constructive conversations
- [Hiring](/career-development/people-management/hiring) - Building great teams
- [Performance Reviews](/career-development/people-management/performance-reviews) - Fair evaluations
- [Team Building](/career-development/people-management/team-building) - Culture and trust

### Team Operations

Running an effective engineering team:

- [Skill Matrices](/career-development/team-operations/skill-matrices) - Tracking team capabilities
- [Training Curation](/career-development/team-operations/training-curation) - Learning and development
- [Capacity Planning](/career-development/team-operations/capacity-planning) - Resource allocation

### Soft Skills

Communication and influence:

- [Technical Writing](/career-development/soft-skills/technical-writing) - Clear documentation
- [Presentations](/career-development/soft-skills/presentations) - Speaking effectively
- [Stakeholder Management](/career-development/soft-skills/stakeholder-management) - Working across functions

## Who This Is For

- **ICs** considering management
- **New managers** learning the ropes
- **Experienced managers** refining their craft
- **Directors+** scaling their impact

## Key Principles

1. **People first** - Your team's success is your success
2. **Continuous learning** - The job changes as you grow
3. **Authenticity** - Lead as yourself, not someone else
4. **Results matter** - Good intentions aren't enough
