# Giving Feedback

Feedback is how people grow. Learning to give it well is a core management skill.

## Types of Feedback

| Type | When | Focus |
|------|------|-------|
| Reinforcing | Catch people doing well | Encourage continuation |
| Redirecting | Behavior needs to change | Specific correction |
| Coaching | Development opportunity | Growth and learning |
